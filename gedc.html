Responsible AI Design Guidance for the Development Sector: landscape review

Produced by the MERL Tech Initiative, 2026

AI governance & guidelines for the development sector are now prolific, yet rarely concern the operational detail of AI tool design from feasibility analysis and funding, to formative research, prototyping, and UX design. At the same time, decades-old principles for participatory research and responsible digital development are being overlooked, even though they still apply. This rapid landscape review maps current formative research & design focused AI guidance - to give AI designers an at-a-glance launchpad for their own AI development processes.

We all know that toolkits and guidelines are not enough: We invite you to tell us what conditions must be met to help implementers make inclusive design of AI tools the norm, not the exception.
